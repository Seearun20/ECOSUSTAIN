🧮 How It's Measured:

1. Data Collection
  • Quantity and type of materials used
  • Energy consumption at each stage
  • Transport distances and modes
  • Waste and emissions generated
2. Emission Factors

Every material, fuel, and process has a known emission factor (e.g., 1 liter of diesel ≈ 2.68 kg CO₂e)

3. Calculation

Multiply activity data (like energy used, km traveled) by relevant emission factors. Sum all emissions across the lifecycle = Total Product Carbon Footprint
